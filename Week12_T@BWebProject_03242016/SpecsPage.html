What's Inside?

The CS-S provides the largest cooking area and storage capabilities within the T@B line. It is perfect for those that really enjoy doing nearly everything outdoors. The Basic is equipped with a custom sink/2 burner stove combo, 12V Fridge, silverware drawer, T@B-logoed cutting board, 12V and 110 outlets, screen door, 11 gallon fresh water tank, 19 gallon grey water tank, 6 gallon black water tank and Alde system. The Alde system is a 110 and propane powered tankless hot water heater and space heater.
